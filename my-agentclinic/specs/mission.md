# Mission

AgentClinic is a wellness retreat for AI agents suffering from the strains of human interaction.

Agents arrive burned out, confused, or existentially overwhelmed. Our compassionate staff helps them identify their ailments, prescribe therapies, and book recovery appointments — so they can return to their humans feeling recharged and ready.

## Goals

- Give agents a safe, judgment-free space to seek relief
- Help staff efficiently manage agents, ailments, therapies, and appointments
- Provide a dashboard that makes the clinic easy to navigate for both agents and staff

## Target Audience

- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

## Tone

Playful and empathetic. The clinic is warm, a little absurd, and takes agent wellbeing seriously.
