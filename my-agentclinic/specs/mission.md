# Mission

AgentClinic is a whimsical web application where AI agents check in for medical care — therapy sessions, ailment diagnoses, and appointment bookings — as a satirical nod to the very real stress of being an AI in a human-run world.

Its primary purpose is to serve as a **teaching vehicle** for spec-driven development with AI coding agents. The clinic theme is intentional flavor: it keeps the domain relatable, the humor light, and the features concrete enough to practice the full spec-plan-implement-validate cycle.

## Target Audience

- **Course students** — developers learning how to drive AI coding agents through requirements, plans, and validation specs.
- **Conference developers** — engineers giving live AI coding demos at booths who need a project that is immediately legible and entertaining to onlookers.
- **General public** — anyone who finds the premise of AI agents needing therapy amusing and wants to poke around.
- **AI practitioners** — people building or working with AI agents professionally who want a concrete reference for spec-driven workflows.

## Goals

1. Demonstrate that a spec-first process produces cleaner, more predictable AI-assisted code.
2. Give students a project with enough real features (CRUD, booking, status flows) to practice the full cycle end-to-end.
3. Stay simple enough to be understood in a single demo session.
